Pizza

Description

A margherita pizza is a simple yet delicious meal. This recipe is very easy for anybody to learn, no matter how much you know about cooking.

Ingredients

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100ml passata
  • handful of grated parmesan
  • handful of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • hanful of basil leaves

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and then stir in the yeast and salt. Pour in 200ml of warm water and olive oil. Stir until you have a soft, fairly wet dough. Knead on a lightly floured surface for 5 minutes. Cover this with a tea towel and set it aside.
  2. Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together. Leave to stand at room temperature.
  3. Roll out the dough, on a floured surface, into two large rounds. These should be approximately 25cm across. The dough need to be very thin. Place these rounds onto two floured baking sheets.
  4. Heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray on the top shelf. Cover the bases with the sauce with the back of a spoon. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season. Put one pizza, still on it's baking sheet on top of the preheated sheet or tray. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Serve with a little more olive oil and basil leaves. Repeat for the remaining pizza.
