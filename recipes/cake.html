Vanilla Cake

Description

This recipe is for a tasty cake, which is very simple to bake.

Ingredients

  • 175g margerine
  • 175g caster sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 175g self-raising flour, sifted
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 180C. Lightly grease an 18cm round cake tin, and cut a piece of greasproof paper to fit the tin.
  2. Put all the ingredients into a bowl and beat with a wooden spoon for 1 minute.
  3. Pour the mixture into the tin, smooth the top and bake on the middle shelf for about 45-50 minutes.
  4. Let the cake sit in the tin for 5 minutes, then run a knife gently around the edge and turn it out onto a wire rack to cool.
